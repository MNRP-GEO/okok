AWESOME FEATURES

We are expertise in Geo-Spatial Data Processing Services, Photogrammetry, LiDAR, Mobile/UAV Processing, GIS Modeling and CAD & GIS Services

Geographical Information System (GIS)

EARTHTRACERS can provide quality, cost-effective GIS and desktop mapping services for a range of applications including asset management, demographic analysis, environmental projects, agribusiness, mineral exploration, mining production and fire management.

We offer data creation, data analysis, digitizing, geospatial analysis, data auditing/cleansing, thematic mapping and 3D data visualisation. We have vast expertise in migrating CAD data to GIS environments and remediating poor quality engineering datasets.

Our consulting services include preparing and implementing enterprise geospatial strategies, linking enterprise software to GIS software and deploying web mapping systems that deliver information across an agency, from customer service to remote field staff.

EARTHTRACERS are experienced in documenting business requirements and assisting with geospatial project design, management and delivery. We also offer a remote GIS bureau service for rural and remote Local Governments that find it difficult to maintain a full time professional role in their organisation.

Remote Sensing (RS)

Whether it is satellite based, airborne or captured using our industrial survey grade drones, we can analyse remotely sensed data to detect and model, environmental health and vigour, operating temperature conditions or conduct volumetric calculations of stockpiles and quarries using a wide variety of technologies.

LiDAR

LiDAR (Light Detection and Ranging) is a Remote Sensing Method that uses light in the from of a pulsed laser to measure ranges (Variable distances) to the Earth.

These light pulses-combined With other data recorded by the airborne system generate precise,three dimensional information about the shape of the earth and it's surface characteristics.

Digital Photogrammetry

The Erathtracers uses the Photgrammetry technique for the compilation of a real terrain into digital maps, this techniques requires a processed output of Aerial images and analytical data's for providing a three dimensional data. This technique is mostly used to minimize the human errors and brings a accurate measurements for land survey.

Photogrammetry provides the elevation data sets like DEM points, Contours and Surface model

Also the processed output data is used for rural to Urban development. Since Urban development i.e., Structural as well as Innovative plans plays a vital role in Physical & economical status of a country with time to time.

Global Navigation Satellite System (GNSS or GPS)

When it comes to mobile mapping, it’s important that your systems are reliable and easy for your fieldworkers to use, without sacrificing project outcomes.

EARTHTRACERS was born in the field. Mobile mapping and GPS data capture are in our DNA so we understand the challenges of working outside in the heat and the cold, in the humid and wet conditions.

UAV Processing

UAVs or Drones have many uses, from simple Aerial Photography & Videography to more complex tasks such as Aerial Surveying & performing close proximity Asset Inspections.

UAV applications and services are only limited by the payloads. With EARTHTRACERS utilising a wide arrange of payloads from RGB, Multispectral & Thermal cameras, we are able to provide a large number of unique services to many industries.

Our recent project experience includes scenic imagery, pest and weed monitoring, 3D terrain modelling and precision horticulture. With a team that holds post-graduate qualifications in remote sensing and hyper-spectral imaging, we’re specialists in processing aerial data into information.
